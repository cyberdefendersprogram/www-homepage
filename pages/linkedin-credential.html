LinkedIn Guide

Add your CyberDefenders credential to LinkedIn.

Use the Licenses & Certifications section so mentors, employers, and admissions reviewers can verify your internship credential.

LinkedIn Fields

Licenses & Certifications

Name CyberDefenders Summer Internship 2026
Issuing organization Growth Sector CyberDefenders Program
Issue date August 2026
Credential ID CDP-2026-001
Credential URL https://cyberdefendersprogram.com/verify/cdp-2026-001/

Where to Add It

  1. Open your LinkedIn profile.
  2. Select Add profile section.
  3. Choose Recommended, then Add licenses & certifications.
  4. Paste your credential fields and verification URL.
